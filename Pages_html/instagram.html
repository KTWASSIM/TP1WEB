instagram

"Instagram, LLC, is a photo and video sharing social networking service owned by the American company Meta Platforms. The platform enables users to upload media, which can be enhanced with filters, organized using hashtags, and tagged with locations through geographical tagging. Posts can be shared publicly or with preapproved followers. Users have the ability to explore other users' content based on tags and locations, discover trending posts, like photos, and follow other users to include their content in a personal feed."
