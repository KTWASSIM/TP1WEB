tiktok

TikTok, known as Douyin in mainland China (Chinese: 抖音; pinyin: Dǒuyīn), is a short-form video hosting service owned by ByteDance. Users can submit videos with durations ranging from 3 seconds to 10 minutes. TikTok and Douyin have achieved global popularity since their launch. By October 2020, TikTok had exceeded 2 billion mobile downloads worldwide. In 2020, Morning Consult recognized TikTok as the third-fastest growing infotech brand, following Zoom and Peacock. In 2021, Cloudflare ranked TikTok as the most popular website, surpassing Google.
